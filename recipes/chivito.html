Chivito

Description

Chivito is one of the bests foods in Uruguay, it's like a beef sandwich with a french fries portion

As well as Milanesa, It could be served in very diferent ways, you can find it in "Al pan" style, or "Al plato" style where chivito is served directly to plate, so any bread is used in this preparation

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Ham
