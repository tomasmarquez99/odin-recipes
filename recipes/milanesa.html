Milanesa

Description

Milanesa is the best dish in the world, it's followed by a big portion of french fries

It could be served in very diferent ways, you can find it in "Al pan" style, or "Napolitana" style where the milanesa is prepared as a pizza with pomodoro salsa on it.

Ingredients

  • Pan rallado
  • Meet
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  1. Cut the meet
  2. Dip the meet in pan rallado
  3. Dip tbe meet again but this time in beaten eggs
  4. Squeeze the meet in pan rallado again
  5. You finished! Milanesa is ready to be in the oven.
