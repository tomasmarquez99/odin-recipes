Pizzeta con faina

Description

Pizza originaly is from Italy, but here in Uruguay, our firsts "Maestros pizzeros" created (or copied from Italy) the Chickpea Faina. Basically, when you order a pizza in Uruguay, usually it's followed by a faina portion.

Pizza's ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Pomodoro salsa
  • Muzzarela
  • Ham

Faina's ingredients

  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chickpea flour
